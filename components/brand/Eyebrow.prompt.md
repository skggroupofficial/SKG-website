**Eyebrow** — DM Mono uppercase micro-label that sits above headings or acts as a wayfinding tag.

```jsx
<Eyebrow dash>Develop · Build · Host</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="muted" size="sm">Phase 01 — Site Acquisition</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="on-dark" dash>Selected Work</Eyebrow>
```

- `dash` adds the brand's leading "—" marker.
- Use `tone="on-dark"` inside Tobacco sections.
